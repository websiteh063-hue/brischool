About the school

A purposeful institution built to shape capable, compassionate, quality individuals.

Founded by Babu Ram Verma, father of Mr. Uma Shanker, the school was established at Vinay Vihar, Sugamau, Indira Nagar, Lucknow with a clear mission: open doors to opportunity through meaningful education.

This value-led learning environment gives children in Lucknow a safe, inspiring path for academic progress, confidence, and character.

Our motive

  • To help every child grow into a total quality person.
  • To develop explorers by nurturing each child's underlying capabilities.
  • To treat curricular and co-curricular activities as essential to complete development.

Our students have excelled in academics, Republic Day Parade participation, annual sports, and cultural events, bringing pride to themselves and to the school community while growing through safe, inspiring school life.
